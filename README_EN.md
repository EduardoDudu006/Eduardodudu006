## ⚡ About me

My name is Eduardo Gonçalves da Luz Silva, I am 40 years old and I am a native and resident of Recife - PE. I completed high school at Escola Professor Ariano Vilar Suassuna. I have a bachelor's degree in Administration from "[Unit-PE](https://pe.unit.br/)", and I am currently studying Systems Analysis and Development at "[Uninassau](https://www.uninassau.edu.br/)". I work in the private security sector and am seeking a career transition from Private Security to Information Technology, which reflects the union between operational maturity and solid technical training. As a graduate in Administration and a student of Systems Analysis and Development, I am seeking to enter the market as a Junior Developer. My technical profile covers the Full-Stack ecosystem, with proficiency in React, React-Native, and Node.js, as well as a solid foundation in Java, Python, and MySQL. This versatility allows me to understand and work in all stages of the software development cycle, from the user interface to data persistence.

---

## 🧩 Differentials

The major competitive differential in my career lies in the behavioral skills honed during my previous professional experience. Working in private security developed in me a high level of resilience and crisis management skills under pressure, essential characteristics for resolving critical system failures. In addition, meticulous attention to detail and operational discipline ensure strict adherence to deadlines and best practices in code architecture. Furthermore, my degree in Business Administration gives me a holistic view of business, allowing me to program solutions with a focus on efficiency and the strategic results of companies.
